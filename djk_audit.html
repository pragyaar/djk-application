What I Found

DJK India's Digital Gaps

A quick outside-in audit of your current digital presence — honest, specific, fixable.

Critical Gap
📉

SEO: You Rank for Your Name. Not Your Category.

A buyer searching "injection molding machine supplier India" or "SMT equipment Gurgaon" likely isn't finding DJK. Your website earns traffic from branded searches — not buyer-intent searches. That's a revenue gap.

Leaking Value
📤

EXPO Leads Go Cold After Day 3

Most B2B companies collect 200–500 leads at an EXPO and send one generic "thanks for visiting" email. By day 5, the lead has moved on. There's no segmented, product-specific follow-up sequence in place — which means money left on the EXPO floor.

Underutilised
💼

LinkedIn Is a Presence, Not a Channel

DJK India is on LinkedIn, but posting inconsistently and mostly company announcements. Your buyers — plant managers, procurement heads, production engineers — are on LinkedIn daily. They need to see thought leadership, not product brochures.

Opportunity
📧

Email List Exists. Automation Doesn't.

There's almost certainly a CRM with hundreds of past contacts. Without segmented email sequences by product line and buyer stage, you're sending the same email to a plant manager and a procurement officer. Personalisation at scale costs nothing once it's set up.

Missing
🎯

No Retargeting After EXPO or Website Visit

When a prospect visits your website or sees your EXPO stand, they should see DJK ads following them on LinkedIn and Google for the next 30 days. Currently, they leave and you disappear. A retargeting setup takes 2 days to build.

Strength
🏭

Strong Product Portfolio. Weak Digital Proof.

DJK has genuine domain authority — Pharma, SMT, Die Casting, Injection Molding across major Indian cities. The product depth is there. The digital proof — case studies, customer success content, deployment stories — isn't surfaced online. Easy win.
