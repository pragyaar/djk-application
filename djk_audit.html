What I found

DJK India's Digital Gaps

An honest outside-in audit of your current digital presence. Six observations — five fixable quickly.

Critical

SEO: You rank for your name. Not your category.

A buyer searching "injection molding machine supplier India" is not finding DJK. You earn traffic on branded searches only. That's a revenue gap hiding in plain sight.

Leaking value

EXPO leads go cold after day three.

Most B2B companies send one generic follow-up email. By day five the lead has moved on. There's no segmented, product-specific sequence in place.

Underutilised

LinkedIn is a presence, not a channel.

Posting inconsistently and mostly announcements. Your buyers — plant managers, procurement heads — are on LinkedIn daily. They need thought leadership, not product brochures.

Opportunity

Email list exists. Automation doesn't.

Without segmented sequences by product line and buyer stage, you're sending the same email to a plant manager and a procurement officer. Personalisation at scale costs nothing once set up.

Missing

No retargeting after EXPO or website visit.

When a prospect visits your website or sees your stand, they should see DJK on LinkedIn and Google for the next 30 days. Currently they leave and you disappear. A retargeting setup takes two days.

Strength

Strong product portfolio. Weak digital proof.

The domain authority is real — Pharma, SMT, Die Casting, Injection Molding across major cities. The case studies and customer stories exist. They just aren't online. Easy win.
